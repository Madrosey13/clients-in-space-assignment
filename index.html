Careers in Space

Pathways into the Future of Space Exploration

Many students hear the phrase "space career" and immediately think of astronauts. Astronauts are important, but they are only one small part of the work required to explore space. Every mission depends on large trams of people with different kinds of knowledge and training. Some design spacecraft. Some build instruments. Some analyze data. Some study human health. Others explain discoveries to the public or make sure complex systems function correctly.
